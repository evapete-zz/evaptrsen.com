App re-design for Homes.com

UI and UX Design

Homes.com, the third-largest real estate portal in the United States, wanted to present themselves as the more dependable alternative to Trulia and Zillow.

  • Role

    UI Designer

  • Project

    Homes.com Mobile App

  • Date

    Mar - Jul 2015

  • Client

    Dominion Enterprises

User onboarding

Over the course of four months, I designed the onboarding experience, key features, pattern library and color palette for the mobile app re-design.

View InVision prototype.

Mortgage calculator UI

This was the first project at Homes.com where I enjoyed full ownership of the product. I worked closely with the product manager to completely re-design the brand’s mortgage calculator.

Color palette

I created the color palette used by the brand today. I also collaborated with designers and developers to create a design system with code components housed in Confluence (not ideal, but Jira was what everytone on the team was accustomed to using).

Results

Homes.com now boasts a highly rated mobile app with an average rating of 4.6 out of 5 on the app store. It is a product people look to us when they are making some of the most important decisions of their lives such as buying a home or investing in a new business opportunity.
